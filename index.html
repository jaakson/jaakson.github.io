About me

Hi! 👋 I'm a Canadian expat living in Bergen, Norway. My academic background is a bit of a patchwork quilt, with swatches drawn from philosophy, logic, mathematics, linguistics, computer science, English literature, and psychology. Throughout my studies, I've kept coming back to methodological questions: how do we do things in this discipline? Are we doing them the right way? If not, what does that say about this field? Are better practices possible? If so, what would they look like? How can we measure improvement here? And can the results of methodological investigations and critiques in one domain shed light on possible limitations how things should (not) be done in other domains?

I love powerlifting, parrots, aroid plants, Wittgenstein, version control, cycling, hair dye, CSS Grid, making and consuming food, making and consuming music, and Amsterdam. 🥰

Experience
